Factorial Infestation

Play as a robot parasite exterminator controlling and building technology inside a factory in real-time to protect a factory tech from being destroyed by the parasitic robots.

Strategize where to place your machines and manage their power consumption, as well as the overall cost of their production.

Trouble Insues!

Around every corner, pests of different shapes, sizes and abilities have started to crawl through factories. Nobody knows where they come from, but the damage they cause can end of being disasterous!

Your Task!

Factories around the block are requesting your assistance in dealing with these parasites. It is your job to make sure every corner of every factory is cleared from these attacks. Destroy them for parts, tamper with their circuitry or otherwise deal with them by any means necessary!
